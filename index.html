Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Boutique Bot application ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our service.

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses Instagram Direct Messages sent to business pages that have authorized our service. This includes:

  • The text content of incoming messages
  • The sender's Instagram user ID
  • Timestamps of messages

2. How We Use This Information

We use the accessed information solely to:

  • Generate automated responses to customer inquiries
  • Notify the page owner when a customer expresses purchase intent
  • Improve the quality of automated responses

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. Message content is processed through AI language model APIs solely for the purpose of generating responses. No personal data is stored permanently.

4. Data Retention

Conversation context is held temporarily in memory during an active session only. No messages or personal information are stored in any database after the conversation ends.

5. Page Owner Authorization

Our application only accesses Instagram pages that have explicitly authorized our service through Meta's official OAuth authorization flow. Page owners can revoke access at any time through their Facebook Settings under Apps and Websites.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including webhook signature validation to ensure all requests originate from Meta's official servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify affected parties of any significant changes.
